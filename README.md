Overview

Hydromobility EV is an electric ride-hailing platform designed to provide clean, affordable, and efficient transportation. The app connects riders with electric vehicle (EV) drivers, helping reduce carbon emissions while offering a smooth and modern mobility experience.

Features

Ride Booking – Request rides quickly with real-time driver matching

Eco-Friendly Transport – 100% electric vehicle fleet

Live Tracking – Track your ride in real time

Secure Payments – Multiple payment options with secure processing

Driver Ratings & Reviews – Rate drivers and improve service quality

Trip History – View past rides and receipts

How It Works

Sign up and create an account

Enter your pickup and destination locations

Choose your ride option

Get matched with a nearby EV driver

Track your ride and enjoy the trip

Pay and rate your experience
